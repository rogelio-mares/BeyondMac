About the Beyond Mac

Learn More About The New Plant Based Burger

The Beyond Mac serves as a tasty, easy, and convenient option for those looking for a vegetarian or plant based meal they can get fast or on the go. The Beyond Mac provides a solution to a problem vegetarians have had for years when looking for good food in a hurry or on the go.

The Beyond Mac also provides a more environmentally friendly option as compared to traditional beef it uses...

  • 89% less greenhouse gas emissions
  • 94% less water
  • 93% less land

Marketing Strategies

We plan to implement both digital and non digital approaches to reach a broad audience while specifically targeting the Beyond Mac to those we feel are most likely to purchase the product. Some of the approaches will include

Paid Product Placement in New York Times Newsletter

We plan to pay for an article to be written about the Beyond Mac in the New York Times newsletter "The Veggie Newsletter".

This will work because it consists of almost all vegetarians reading the article, and 92% of NYT readers are liberals meaning they are more likely to try and repurchase the Beyond Mac.

Location Based Search Ads

Using location data, we can display ads for the Beyond Mac exclusively to users looking for food within a 3 mile radius of any McDonalds for increased convenience.

Using Googles ability to track demographics we can further narrow this down to target those who have recently done searches for vegetarian meal options.

Blind Taste Test Challenge

This consists of a promotion targeted towards non-vegetarians to expand our target market.

For this challenge we anonymously package both a beef burger and a Beyond Mac to allow customers to guess for themselves which is which and make an unbiased decision as to which burger they prefer.

This also can be featured as a short formatted social media trend challenge to then be uploaded to Tik Tok, Instagram, and Youtube by customers for their own viral benefit.
